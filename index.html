Greetings to you fellow creator or a person whose interst I piqued. My name is Stefan Bogdanov and I
am one of the most enthusiastic and passionate designers of a generation. A lot would argue it is a bold
statement and I agree. My field of expertiese revolves around a vast variaty of softwares needed for cinematics
and videogame production. Working on projects with different sorts of people, from artists to programers, from
extraodinary to those who have work related problems, I have accumulated a lot of knowledge regarding all of
the phases of a workflow and how to handle difficult situation, be it project or human related and therefore I
came to love working in teams with others. My goal is to use my creativity, knowledge and experience to bring a
lot of my ideas to life and help people realise their own ideas and dreams.
