Profile info
Greetings to you fellow creator or a person whose interst I piqued. My name is Stefan Bogdanov and I am one
of the most enthusiastic and passionate designers of a generation. A lot would argue it is a bold statement and I agree.
My field of expertiese revolves around a vast variaty of softwares needed for cinematics and videogame production.
Working on projects with different sorts of people, from artists to programers, from extraodinary to those who have
work related problems, I have accumulated a lot of knowledge regarding all of the phases of a workflow and how to
handle difficult situation, be it project or human related and therefore I came to love working in teams with others. My
goal is to use my creativity, knowledge and experience to bring a lot of my ideas to life and help people realise their own
ideas and dreams.
Projects and works
Early Beginnings. In my early days I have done a lot of drawings and illustrations that represented some story I have
written or read. This has gotten me interested in a narative aspect of video games and movies, and I started carring for
details when making ideas and projects. https://kyadai.github.io/ArtworksStories/
Present Day. As the creating phase ends so must realisation of those ideas begin. I have compiled a list of projects that I
have worked on by myself or with others.
Sir Willhelm. This is the first time I have tried to recreate one of my personal characters. Greatly inspired by From
Software estetic and story, I tackled in using Zbrush for the first time and tried learning it from scrach in a short period of
time to bring this character to life. https://www.artstation.com/artwork/3o2nJm
Call of Cthulhu. I’ve been reading a lot of H.P. Lovecraft’s work and I’ve been amazed by the concept of cosmic horror
and fear of unknown. As such I wanted to pay tribute to one of his iconic characters, Cthulhu. This is the first time I came
in contact with complete workflow of a character creation and animation in general.https://kyadai.github.io/Cthulhu/
Storyboard making. Rather soon I started falling in love with creating ideas and stories. And as such we had a task to
recreate a given story. Although we had to keep the core concept of a story, I’ve been given permision to apply changes
to it. That said I had to reimagine and rewrite the script, while also making it easier enough for the artist I worked with
to make it look polished enough even though it’s only a storyboard cinematic. https://kyadai.github.io/Poltergaist/
Kings Squable. This is an idea I’ve had for a long time, but only now had time and knowledge to bring it to realisation.
On this project I worked with my dear friend and collegue who created and programed a logic behind a turn-based
strategy game. Together the the of us managed to create and upgrade a modest beginning of said idea and learn how
making a game looks like in Unreal Engine 5. https://dusan-blip.github.io/KingsSquable/
Future Plans. Game Design is my passion and I feel like my creativity can and will create and help in creating a lot of
beautiful ideas. I still have much to learn in order to bring my dreams into reality, I currently work on a drawn animation
with a few artists and on one major 3D animated short movie because I want to tackle all forms of entertainment.
